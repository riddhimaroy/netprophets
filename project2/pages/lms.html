LMS

The NetProphets Learning Management solution and services offers both a corporate training platform as well as integration and support for Canvas LMIS customers. The NP-LMS is designed to create, manage, deliver, and track corporate training programs, vocational development and skill development initiatives.

Canvas Integration and Support Services

We recognize the complexities of managing a Canvas LMS, especially when Educational Teams lack the technical skills and resources in managing learning platforms. That’s why we offer dedicated Canvas LMS support to help you enhance the efficiency and functionality of your learning management system.

Our services include comprehensive student and faculty support, assisting them with their daily educational tasks and preventing delays caused by limited in-house support.

Our expert team brings deep knowledge and proven expertise to keep your LMS running smoothly, allowing you to focus on delivering outstanding educational experiences without technical challenges. From complex system configurations and integrations to troubleshooting and user training, NP Technology and its partners are here to provide dependable and professional Canvas LMS support.

Let us handle the technical details, so you can concentrate on what matters most—educating students.

NP-LMS Professional Education Solution

The advantage over SaaS platforms is its customizable approach to personalize learning and streamline delivery, administration, and analytics of the educational and training programs.

LMS Key Features

Supports different course formats : weekly, topic-based, social, and custom formats.

Monitoring learners' progress and generating performance reports.

Custom reports to track performance, participation, and other key metrics specific to needs.

Supports a large number of users and courses, suitable for both small and large organizations. API-based integrations can also connect your custom LMS with third-party apps for data sharing.

Distribution of multimedia content, documents, and interactive activities.

That suits different institutional and corporate needs and course structures.

A formal review system and approval workflow, where designated reviewers can evaluate courses for quality and content.

Customized grading methods, weighted categories to reflect course importance, grade history, anonymisation of student identities.

The LMS mobile app extends learning and course management to smartphones and tablets, enabling on-the-go access for both learners and instructors.

Management of content, access control, customized courseware delivery and permission based Trainer activity.

Enables institutions and Companies to publish, manage, and list courses under their specific branding and guidelines.

Facilitating interactions between learners and instructors via messaging, forums, and video conferencing.

Tools for quizzes, exams, and assignments with automated grading.

Such as Forums, Chatroom and Shared spaces that promote group learning.

Student course ratings; customizable instructor feedback; analytics tools to track feedback trends.
