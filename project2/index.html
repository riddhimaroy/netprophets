About Us

Visionaries in Technology, Trusted by Business

Founded by Grant Koops, NetProphets Technology was inspired by his experience with NetProphets Cyberworks (NPC) in India. There, he collaborated with a 950+ member team driving innovation and social impact through partnerships with the Government of India.

Grant launched NetProphets Technology to bring NPC’s expertise to the U.S., delivering accessible solutions that foster growth, efficiency, and meaningful change for businesses and communities.

Mission

To empower businesses by providing advanced, reliable, customer-focused technology solutions, fostering innovation and growth.

Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative technology solutions, renowned for delivering results and driving transformative change for our clients.

Value

We stay ahead in a fast-changing tech world, guided by trust, transparency, and ethics, dedicated to excellence in every project, with your growth driving our success.

Meet the Team

Grant Koops

I'm Grant, an entrepreneur and business development professional with a bachelor’s degree in Entrepreneurship & Finance from Trinity Christian College. At Trinity, I played a key role in launching Vermeer Studios, a business incubator designed to empower students and community members to pursue their entrepreneurial dreams. I developed programs, facilitated mentorship opportunities, and leveraged the college’s network to provide aspiring entrepreneurs with the resources and support needed to build and grow their ventures.

Following this, I founded NetProphets Technology, a brand extension of NetProphets Cyberworks, a leading tech company based in Delhi, India. My three-week immersion in India, working closely with the NetProphets team, deepened my understanding of global business and inspired me to foster meaningful connections between West Michigan and Delhi through innovative technology initiatives.

With a passion for entrepreneurship, strategic networking, and cross-cultural collaboration, I’m dedicated to driving growth and creating opportunities for businesses and communities
